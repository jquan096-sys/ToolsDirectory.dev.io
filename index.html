Real Estate in Cambodia 2025

Cambodia's property market continues to grow as one of Southeast Asia's most dynamic real estate destinations. With no foreign ownership restrictions on condominiums and a rapidly expanding middle class, both local and international buyers are finding exceptional value.

Phnom Penh remains the most active market, offering everything from luxury penthouses on Koh Pich (Diamond Island) to affordable serviced apartments in BKK1 and Toul Tom Poung. Rental yields of 6–9% annually make buy-to-let investment particularly attractive.

Siem Reap is experiencing a tourism-driven revival with villa rentals near Angkor Wat. Sihanoukville and the South Coast offer beachfront land and resort development opportunities at competitive prices.

Foreign buyers can legally own condominiums (strata title) up to 70% of any building. Land must be held through a Cambodian company or long-term lease. Q Property connects you with experienced legal advisors who guide the process.
